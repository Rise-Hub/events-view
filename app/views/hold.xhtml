  • Consumers

    Wherever you are, the web‑based discovery app will allow you to find the best independent coffee in town, rated by baristas.

  • Baristas

    As well as advertising coffee shops and events, our discovery platform personalises this experience through our profile cards. These are unique linkable pages where baristas can provide career profiles, personal experiences, and can find work.

  • Coffee Houses

    Our partner coffee houses get a modern and portable point-of-sale (PoS) and back-office management system that directly links to suppliers and the customer, to manage stock levels, sales, and customer loyalty.

  • Roasters

    We know the location of the farms, the market prices, and the demand from the coffee shops. We provide roasters with that information so they can provide better quality products that match the seasonal changes and time-consuming investments needed.
